Healthy foods

Healthy foods are foods that provide your body with the nutrients it needs to function well, maintain energy, and prevent diseases. They are typically rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and protein while being low in unhealthy fats, added sugars, and excessive salt. Eating healthy foods helps support overall health, proper growth, and long-term wellness.

Some examples of healthy foods include: Fruits: Apples, bananas, berries, oranges Vegetables: Spinach, broccoli, carrots, peppers Whole grains: Brown rice, oats, whole wheat bread Lean proteins: Chicken, fish, beans, tofu Healthy fats: Nuts, seeds, avocado, olive oil Dairy (or alternatives): Yogurt, milk, cheese
